About me

Not a master
of one —
a student
of all.

I'm Tanveer, based in India. They say "jack of all trades, master of none" — but honestly? I wear that with pride. My curiosity doesn't stop at one door.

I sketch when an idea strikes, dive into tech when something sparks, and spend every spare moment learning something I didn't know yesterday. Art meets code in my world, and that intersection is where I feel most alive.

Sketching & Drawing

Pen to paper before pixels. I sketch ideas, characters, concepts, and whatever my hand feels like expressing at 2am. Drawing is where my thinking begins.

Tech & Tinkering

I love breaking things to understand them, then building them back better. From code to gadgets, if it's technical and interesting, I want to know how it works.

Creative Problem Solving

The sketch-to-tech crossover is where I shine. I approach problems visually first — drawing out the solution — before writing a single line of logic.

04

Perpetual Learning

This isn't a hobby — it's a philosophy. Every week I pick something new to study: a language, a tool, a craft, a concept. Progress over perfection, always.

Visual Thinking

I communicate in visuals — diagrams, doodles, wireframes. Whether explaining an idea or exploring one, putting it on paper makes it real and actionable.
